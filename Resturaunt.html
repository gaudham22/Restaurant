"WHO WE ARE?"

Welcome to FOOD LOVER, our home located in INDIA, where we invite guests to celebrate life and share sundry blends of modern and traditional Indian cuisine. Our goal is to unify our guest’s cultures and spirits with our own, expressing passion and generosity in the cuisine we share. Flavorful herbs and spices combine textures to appease broad-based palates. Our attentive staff ensures that your entire dining experience at FOODLOVER is stellar from beginning to end. Each passing of our shared plates and the breaking of fresh Indian food opens up a new conversation.

Whether part of a party of fifty or a party of two, your experience throughout our elegant, yet comfortable space is eventful. We are sharing our long held Indian traditions with a modern contemporary twist. You will feel both close to home and apart of something new.
